## Project Overview

**New Braunfels ISD Administration Building** renovation project modernizes the district's central administrative facility, creating an efficient, collaborative, and welcoming environment for district leadership, support staff, and the public. The project transforms a 1980s-era building into a contemporary administrative hub with improved workflows, technology integration, and public-facing service areas. The design demonstrates best practices in office ergonomics, collaboration, and accessibility.

**Status**: Construction | **Phase**: 75% Complete | **Team Lead**: Zhun Jiao, Architect

## Renovation Strategy

The project addresses building systems, layouts, and public experience:

**Building Systems Upgrade**
- HVAC replacement with smart controls and zone-based heating/cooling
- Electrical system upgrade for modern IT and communication infrastructure
- Plumbing and restroom modernization with water-efficient fixtures
- Roof replacement with solar-ready membrane
- Window replacement (energy-efficient, improved daylighting)
- Security system integration (access control, camera surveillance)

**Interior Renovation**
- Open office layout with flexibility for collaboration and focused work
- Modern finishes including sustainable materials (low-VOC paint, reclaimed wood)
- Technology-enabled meeting and conference spaces
- Public service areas with improved wayfinding and accessibility
- Administrative support areas with ergonomic furniture
- Break rooms and wellness spaces for staff

## Functional Organization

The space reorganizes workflows and improves service delivery:

- **Ground Floor**: Public services, waiting areas, meeting rooms, records management
- **First Floor**: Executive offices, board room, conference spaces, planning areas
- **Second Floor**: Department offices (HR, Finance, Operations, IT), shared collaboration zones
- **Support Spaces**: Copier rooms, break areas, storage, loading dock
- **Technology**: Fiber-optic network, wireless throughout, smart building automation

## Public Experience

Improved customer service and accessibility:

- Modern, welcoming entry with clear wayfinding
- Accessible public service counters (ADA compliant, multiple stations)
- Waiting areas with comfortable seating and visual interest
- Multiple language signage and materials
- Secure but transparent board meeting area for public observation
- Parking with accessible and safe pedestrian pathways

## Employee Wellness

The renovation prioritizes staff health and productivity:

- Natural daylighting and views (75% of spaces)
- Ergonomic work stations with adjustable heights and chairs
- Wellness room for meditation, nursing, or rest
- Break room with nutrition education and healthy snacks
- Outdoor patio with natural plantings
- Flexible scheduling-friendly spaces (hot-desking options)

## Sustainability Features

**LEED Silver Certification** with operational cost savings:

- Energy: High-efficiency HVAC, LED lighting, occupancy controls (40% energy reduction target)
- Water: Low-flow fixtures, rainwater for landscape irrigation
- Materials: Sustainable selection, low-VOC finishes, waste diversion
- Indoor Environmental Quality: Daylighting, natural ventilation, air quality monitoring
- Operations: Building automation, energy monitoring dashboard
