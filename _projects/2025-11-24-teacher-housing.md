## Project Overview

Teacher Housing Community addresses the critical shortage of affordable housing for educators in Central Texas. This 80-unit townhouse development provides quality, secure housing designed specifically for teachers' needs and budgets.

## Design Features

- **80 townhouse units** with 2-3 bedroom floor plans
- **Affordability targeting**: 60-80% AMI (Area Median Income)
- **Unit amenities**: 
  - Modern open-plan kitchens
  - In-unit washer/dryer
  - Private patios and small outdoor spaces
  - Fiber-ready for home office/teaching
- **Community spaces**:
  - Central green with gathering pavilion
  - Playgrounds and recreational areas
  - Community garden plots
  - Fitness center in main building

## Financial Model

- **Total development cost**: $18M ($225k per unit)
- **Funding**: Mix of HUD/tax credits and conventional financing
- **Affordability period**: 40 years
- **Target tenants**: K-12 teachers, administrators, support staff
- **Income replacement**: Tax credits + resident income = 6.5% blended return

## Sustainability & Wellness

- Energy-efficient HVAC and water systems
- Bike storage and EV charging at 20% of units
- Native landscaping requiring minimal irrigation
- Sound insulation between units for quiet living environments
- Access to transit lines and nearby schools
