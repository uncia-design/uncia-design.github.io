## Project Overview

Round Rock City Center is a comprehensive mixed-use development designed to transform a central Austin-area site into a vibrant urban destination. The project integrates residential, retail, and office components with public gathering spaces and sustainable landscape design.

## Design Concept

The development features:
- **180 residential units** across three mid-rise towers with modern amenities
- **25,000 sq ft retail/restaurant** ground floor with active street frontage
- **50,000 sq ft office space** designed for tech and creative companies
- **Central plaza** with community gathering spaces, water features, and landscaping
- **Integrated parking** below grade with pedestrian-only upper levels
- **LEED certification** targeting with sustainable building materials and systems

## Financial Analysis

- **Total project cost**: $120M
- **Projected completion**: 2026
- **ROI**: 8.2% annually with mixed-use revenue streams
- **Occupancy assumptions**: Residential 85%, Retail 90%, Office 80%

## Sustainable Features

- Rainwater collection and greywater recycling
- Native plant landscaping reducing irrigation by 40%
- Solar canopies in parking areas
- EV charging infrastructure for 15% of parking spaces
- District heating/cooling system reducing energy use by 25%
